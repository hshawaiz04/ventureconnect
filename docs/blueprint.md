# **App Name**: VentureConnect

## Core Features:

- User Registration/Login: Secure registration and login functionality for all user roles (Investors, Business People, Bankers, Business Advisors) with role selection on the landing page.
- Business Idea Posting: Allow business people to post their business proposals/ideas with detailed descriptions and contact information.
- Investor Proposal Posting: Allow investors to post their investment interests and contact information.
- Loan Details Posting: Allow bankers to post loan details and offerings for businesses.
- Business Advice Forum: Enable business advisors to post informative articles and solutions to user queries.
- Proposal Search and Filtering: Enable users to search and filter business proposals, investor proposals, and loan details based on categories and keywords. Index business proposals in Firestore.
- Subscription and Payment: Implement a subscription and payment system for accessing the platform's features (charging fees from both investors and business people).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark green (#006400) for a sense of growth, wealth, and stability.
- Secondary color: Black (#000000) for a professional and sophisticated feel.
- Accent color: Light gray (#D3D3D3) for a clean and modern contrast, ensuring readability and balance.
- Body and headline font: 'Roboto', a neo-grotesque sans-serif font that provides a clean, readable, and modern look.
- Use modern and professional icons representing various business categories and investment opportunities.
- Design a clean and intuitive layout with clear sections for business proposals, investor profiles, and resources. The landing page should provide clear options for user role selection (Investor, Business Person, etc.).
- Subtle animations and transitions for a smooth user experience.
